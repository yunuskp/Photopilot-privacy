Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 5, 2026

PhotoPilot ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how PhotoPilot handles information when you use our iPhone application.

1. Information We Collect

PhotoPilot is designed to help users review and clean up screenshots, videos, and similar photos from their photo library.

We may access the following only with your permission:

  • Photos and videos from your device photo library
  • Subscription status related to in-app purchases

We do not require account creation.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information only to provide the app’s features, including:

  • Reviewing screenshots
  • Identifying large videos
  • Finding similar photos
  • Showing cleanup suggestions
  • Managing premium subscription access

3. Photo Library Access

PhotoPilot requests access to your photo library only so the app can analyze and display items for cleanup purposes.

Your photos and videos are processed on-device unless otherwise stated in a future app update.

We do not sell your photos, and we do not use your photos for advertising.

4. In-App Purchases

PhotoPilot offers auto-renewable subscriptions for premium features. Purchases and subscription management are handled by Apple through the App Store.

We do not collect or store your payment card details.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

We do not share your personal photos or videos with third parties except where required by law or necessary for Apple in-app purchase functionality.

6. Data Retention

PhotoPilot does not maintain a user account database for your personal photo library content.

If the app stores temporary app data or cached results on your device, that information remains on your device unless removed by you or deleted by the app.

7. Children’s Privacy

PhotoPilot is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user information and rely on Apple’s platform security features where applicable.

9. Your Choices

  • Deny or revoke photo library access in iPhone Settings
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID subscription settings
  • Delete the app and its local data from your device

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this page with an updated effective date.
